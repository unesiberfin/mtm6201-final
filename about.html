About Our Recreation Center

Dedicated to promoting health, wellness, and community engagement through quality recreational programs and facilities.

Our Story

Founded in 1985, our recreation center has been serving the community for over 40 years. What started as a small facility with basic amenities has grown into a comprehensive center offering diverse programs for all ages. Our mission is to enhance the quality of life for all community members by providing accessible, affordable, and high-quality recreational opportunities that promote physical and mental well-being. We believe in fostering a sense of community and belonging, where everyone feels welcome regardless of age, ability, or background.

Our Facilities

Aquatic Center

Olympic-sized swimming pool, water slide, kiddie pool, and hot tub. Perfect for swimmers of all ages and abilities.

Fitness Center

State-of-the-art cardio and strength equipment, free weights area, and dedicated functional training space.
